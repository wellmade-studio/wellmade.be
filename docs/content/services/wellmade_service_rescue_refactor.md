## Stuck? We unstick things.

Product broken. Team gone. Technical debt strangling every new feature.

We've seen it. We fix it.

---

## Why it matters.

Sometimes the best path forward isn't a new build. It's saving what you have.

You've invested time, money, users. Walking away isn't always the answer. But neither is pretending everything is fine while the codebase rots.

We help you figure out what's worth saving - and actually save it.

---

## What it looks like.

**Assessment.** We dig in. Codebase, infrastructure, architecture, processes. We find out what's broken, what's fragile, and what's actually fine.

**Honest diagnosis.** Sometimes it's fixable. Sometimes it's "burn it down and start over." We'll tell you which, and why.

**Stabilization.** Stop the bleeding. Fix the critical bugs. Shore up security. Get things running reliably again.

**Refactoring.** Untangle the mess. Pay down technical debt. Make the codebase something a team can actually work with.

**Knowledge recovery.** The original team left? We document what we find. We rebuild the tribal knowledge that walked out the door.

**Handover or continue.** We can hand it back to your team, help you hire, or stick around to keep building.

- Cleaner codebase with less debt
