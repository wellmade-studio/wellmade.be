# Partnerships

---

## We don't do subcontracting.

We do partnerships.

The difference matters.

Subcontracting is: here's a spec, build it, send the invoice.

Partnership is: we're in this together. Your client is our client. Your reputation is our reputation.

---

## How it works.

We integrate with your team. Not as a black box delivering code - as builders who understand your client, your context, your constraints.

We join calls when it makes sense. We challenge ideas when needed. We bring the craft - and sometimes the vision too.

You stay in control of the relationship. We stay focused on the product.

---

## Clear roles, mutual trust.

Good partnerships need clarity.

Who owns what. Who talks to whom. Who decides when there's disagreement.

Get the RACI right and we're in. Skip it and we'll both regret it.

---

## What we bring.

- **Builders.** Senior people who ship. No juniors learning on your client's budget.
- **Speed.** AI-native methods mean we move fast without cutting corners.
- **Transparency.** You'll always know where things stand.
- **Flexibility.** Scale up, scale down. Project-based or ongoing.

---

## What we need.

- **Trust.** Let us do what we're good at.
- **Access.** To real context, real users when possible, real stakes.
- **Honesty.** If something's off, say it early.

---

## Who we work with.

Agencies. Studios. Consultancies. Innovation teams.

Anyone who needs builders but doesn't want to roll the dice on freelancers or fight with offshore teams.

If you have the client and the vision, we have the craft.
